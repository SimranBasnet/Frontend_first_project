Our Story

Aarohan began with a simple idea: bridging the gap between Fashion and the global demand for unique, handcrafted products. Founded by a team passionate about sustainability, we work closely with weavers, designer, and craft communities across the country to bring you timeless designs.

Each item in our collection tells a story — not just of style,comfort, and love for handcraft. At Aarohan, you don’t just buy a product; you support a fashion.
